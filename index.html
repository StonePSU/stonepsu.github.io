about me

I graduated from The Pennsylvania State University in May 2000 with a Bachelor of Science in Management Science and Information Systems. My passion for learning has recently led me to earn my Front-End Developer Certificate from FreeCodeCamp's online code bootcamp.
An IT professional with over 15 years of experience, I spent a number of years consulting in the area of Customer Relationship Management (CRM) and Marketing Automation. I've implemented software for a number of clients across a wide variety of industries.
Now I'm an aspiring front-end web developer with experience in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap and more. I'm also experienced in using Git and Github for version control and collaboration.
